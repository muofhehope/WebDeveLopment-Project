The best movies according to Muofhe

My top 5 movies of all times.

Daddy's little girls

My favourite drama movies when a mechanic and his family are struggling to make ends meet with his ex-girlfriend and her drug dealing boyfriend. He struggles to get custody of his children.

Purple hurts

My favourite romance/musical movie. Spite of their many differences. A struggling singer-songwriter and Luke, a troubled Marine, agree to marry solely for military benefits.

The bad guys

A gang of notorious animal criminals pretends to seek being rehabilitated, only for their leader to realize that he genuinely wants to change his ways.

The pursuit of happyness

A struggling salesman takes custody of his son as he's poised to begin a life-changing professional career.

Ready or not

bride's wedding night takes a sinister turn when her eccentric new in-laws force her to take part in a terrifying game.
